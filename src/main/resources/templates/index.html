Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

How do I sign up?

Click on the "Sign Up" button in the navigation bar and fill in your details. You'll need to provide your full name, username, email, and password.

How many books can I borrow?

Students can borrow multiple books at once. The exact limit depends on your institution's policy. Check with your librarian for specific details.

What happens if I return a book late?

Late returns incur fines that are automatically calculated. You can view and pay fines through your student dashboard.

Can I renew a book?

Yes! You can renew books up to 2 times through your student dashboard, as long as the book is not overdue and no one else has reserved it.
